Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 28, 2026

Harmony Hub is a private family app. We do not collect, sell, or share your personal data with anyone.

1. What Harmony Hub Is

Harmony Hub is a private household management app built for personal family use. It helps one household track bills, chores, events, and lists. It is not a commercial product offered to the general public.

2. Data Storage

All app data (bills, chores, events, lists, family profiles) is stored exclusively in your browser's localStorage on your own device. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not transmitted to any server
  • Is not accessible to us or any third party
  • Can be cleared at any time by clearing your browser data

3. Google Calendar Integration

Harmony Hub offers an optional Google Calendar integration. If you choose to connect Google Calendar:

  • You will be asked to sign in with your Google account via Google's own OAuth 2.0 flow
  • We request read-only access to your Google Calendar events
  • Your Google access token is stored only in your browser's localStorage
  • Calendar event data fetched from Google is cached in your browser's localStorage
  • No Google account data is sent to, stored on, or processed by any Harmony Hub server
  • You can disconnect Google Calendar at any time from within the app

Our use of Google Calendar data complies with the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

4. Cookies & Tracking

Harmony Hub does not use cookies, analytics, tracking pixels, or any third-party tracking technology. We do not track usage patterns, page views, or user behavior.

5. Children's Privacy

Harmony Hub is a private family tool. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children.

6. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated date at the top of this page will reflect the change. Since this is a private family app, significant changes are unlikely.
